Introduction 

   

Jelise is currently a 3 part comic series on my instagram.

 Jelise is a comic series created by Lana Bydder. It is about a 15 year old girl called Jessica Swan and her journey through highschool. She faces a lot of difficulties but makes it through. This website covers the main Jelise cast: Jessica, Elise and James through their evolutions as character.
